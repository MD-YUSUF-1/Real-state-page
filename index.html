What services do you offer?

We provide a full range of building and construction services, including residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Our services cover everything from initial design and planning to construction management, material procurement, and final inspections.

Do you provide warranties for your work?

Yes, we offer warranties on both labor and materials used in our projects. The duration and terms of the warranty will vary depending on the type of work and materials, but it typically covers structural issues and defects in craftsmanship for a specified period.

What is included in a construction contract?

A construction contract typically includes a detailed description of the project scope, timeline, payment schedule, materials to be used, responsibilities of both parties, change orders, and conditions for any delays or disputes. It protects both the client and contractor by clearly defining expectations and deliverables.

How do I start a construction project with you?

To start a project, the first step is to contact us for a consultation. During this meeting, we’ll discuss your project goals, timeline, budget, and specific requirements. Afterward, we’ll provide a proposal and a detailed contract for review.

How is the cost of a construction project determined?

Project costs are calculated based on factors such as the scope of work, materials used, labor required, and project complexity. We provide detailed estimates after discussing your requirements. We also offer transparent cost breakdowns, so you understand exactly where your investment is going.
